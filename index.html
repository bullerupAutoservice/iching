I Ching.

The I Ching has served for thousands of years as a philosophical taxonomy of the universe, a guide to an ethical life, a manual for rulers, and an oracle of one’s personal future and the future of the state.

BEFORE YOU CONSULT THE ORACLE, YOU NEED TO HAVE A QUESTION YOU WANT TO ASK. Dont start casting until you've done this.

It is highly recommended to read the about page before consulting the oracle. You find that in the top left corner.

Consulting the I Ching without knowing what you're asking is doomed for confusion.

CAST HEXAGRAM¨

¨This is the overall scenario of your dilemma.

CHANING LINES¨

¨This represents the change in your cast hexagram. Each line can be solid or changing. The change will determine the transformed hexagram. The prevailing line shows a commentary to the cast hexagram.

TRANSFORMED HEXAGRAM¨

¨The transformed hexagram describes the future situation, after the lines have changed and if you follow the advice the I Ching has given.
